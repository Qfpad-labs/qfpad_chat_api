Introducing QPAD Fiesta

The QPAD sale includes two promotional incentives for early participants.

1. $1,000 USDC Community Draw
- Users who invest $250 USDC or more in the presale are eligible.
- Users should keep proof of entry, specifically the transaction hash.
- All eligible entries go into a spin-the-wheel draw.
- One winner receives $1,000 USDC.

2. Whale Rebates
- Only 20 slots are available.
- The max buy remains $1,600 USDC.
- Eligible whale buyers receive 6.25% cashback in USDC.
- Eligible whale buyers also receive a 5% bonus QPAD allocation.
