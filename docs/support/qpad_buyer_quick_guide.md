QPAD Presale Quick Buying Guide

Tip: use a laptop if you can. Once MetaMask connects, the rest is smooth.

What you need before you start:
- An Ethereum wallet (MetaMask or any WalletConnect wallet) with USDC and a little ETH for gas on Ethereum mainnet.
- A QF wallet (SubWallet or Talisman). You only need its SS58 address. You do not have to connect it to buy.

Steps:

1. Open https://qfpad.xyz/projects and click the QPAD presale.
2. Connect your Ethereum wallet. Make sure it is on Ethereum mainnet.
3. Get your QF SS58 address from SubWallet or Talisman. Copy it.
4. In the QF claim address field, paste the SS58 address you just copied. This is the QF wallet that will receive your QPAD.
5. Enter the USDC amount you want to contribute.
6. Click Approve USDC and confirm in your Ethereum wallet.
7. Click Contribute and confirm the second transaction in your Ethereum wallet.
8. Wait for the page to update once the background worker confirms your purchase and registers your allocation on QF Network.
9. When claims open, come back, connect SubWallet or Talisman, and click Claim QPAD.

Troubleshooting:
- Make sure the SS58 address is the QF wallet you actually control. QPAD will be claimable by that wallet only.
- If a transaction fails, check that you have enough USDC and ETH for gas, and that you are on Ethereum mainnet.
- If the page seems stuck after paying, refresh. Your purchase is on-chain and the worker will pick it up.
